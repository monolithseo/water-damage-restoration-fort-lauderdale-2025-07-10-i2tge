Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond to water damage emergencies?

We provide 24/7 emergency response and typically arrive within 60 minutes of your call. Our rapid response helps minimize damage and prevents secondary issues like mold growth.

What should I do while waiting for your team to arrive?

If safe to do so, turn off the water source and electricity to affected areas. Remove valuable items and document the damage with photos. Avoid entering areas with structural damage or electrical hazards.

How do you handle insurance claims?

We work directly with all major insurance providers and handle the entire claims process. Our team documents all damage thoroughly, provides detailed reports, and communicates directly with your insurance company.

How long does the restoration process take?

The timeline varies depending on the extent of damage, but most residential projects are completed within 3-5 days. We provide detailed timelines and keep you updated throughout the process.

What types of water damage do you handle?

We handle all types of water damage including burst pipes, flooding, sewage backups, storm damage, and appliance leaks. Our team is certified to handle both clean water and contaminated water situations.
